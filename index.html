Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suboder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication in Chameleons
  • Most of the chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to rap up around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intellegent animals
  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigatianal abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  • Most have red-orange eyes.
  • Female Cicadas may be attracted to the sound of motors
